Working with Negative Numbers!

Simply find the binary representation of the positive number, and then you flip all the bits and add one to the result.

The most significant bit actually has a negative weight.

Consider this!
The leftmost bit acts as the "sign bit"; a sign bit of 1 means the number will be negative, and sign bit of 0 means the number is zero or positive.
